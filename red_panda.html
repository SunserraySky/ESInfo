Red Panda

Red panda, ailurus fulgens, part of the red pandas family.
Red pandas are very endangered, they have a population of less than 10,000 in the wild.
They have a lifespan of 12 to 14 years.
Red pandas live in the Eastern Himalayas, China, Nepal, and Bhutan.
hey have an extra thumb so they can grab bamboo stems and tree branches.
Red pandas are herbivores, they eat bamboo, roots, succilent grasses, fruits, insects, and grubs.
