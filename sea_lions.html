Sea Lions

Sea lions, otariinae, part of the eared seal family.
Sea lions are very endangered and have a population of 10,000 individuals left.
They have a lifespan of 20 to 30 years.
Sea lions are found in all waters except Northern Atlantic Ocean.
Their fur can help them retain a high body temperature in frigid waters.
Sea lions are carnivores, they eat herring salmon, krill, squid, and octopus.
